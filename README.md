# Food-Delivery-Service-Efficiency-Analysis

we have an Food delivery companies lose profit when orders are late. This project involves analyzing a 
real-world dataset of delivery orders to understand the root causes of delays. 
The goal is to provide data-driven insights that can help the company enhance its service 
quality, improve customer satisfaction, and increase profitability.
